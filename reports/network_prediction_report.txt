3. HEALTH SCORE EXPLANATION
---------------------------
The calculated 'network_health_score' ranges from 0 (critical fault) to 100 (optimal health).
It normalizes and aggregates multiple physical and networking dimensions:
  * Battery Level (35%): Measures energy reserves directly.
  * Signal Strength (25%): Linear normalization of RSSI from [-100, -30] dBm.
  * Latency (20%): Linear normalization of latency from [0, 1500] ms where lower is healthier.
  * Packet Loss (20%): Linear normalization of packet loss rate from [0, 100]% where lower is healthier.

Formula:
  network_health_score = 0.35*battery_norm + 0.25*signal_norm + 0.20*latency_norm + 0.20*loss_norm
